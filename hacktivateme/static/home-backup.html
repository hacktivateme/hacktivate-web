We are Hacktivate.

The high stakes, pressure driven environment of Hacktivate events is perfect for encouraging developers and designers to work quickly and carefully together to achieve success. Problems are structured in such a way that teams must work together, and can not simply rest on one ‘star’ to do the hard yards.

In practice, this translates well into industry - a team is the sum of its parts, and, as cliche as it may sound, is only as strong as its weakest link. By encouraging development as a team sport, we help competitors nurture skills transferable to whatever challenges they may face in the workplace.
